A provisional patent application was filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office in 2025 covering the core architecture of the Vocarum consensus journalism system.

The application covers the multi-model intersection pipeline — in which multiple independent AI models interpret the same source material and the areas of convergence and divergence are systematically mapped — along with the distributed validation approach and the tamper-evident signed manifests used for provenance tracking and verifiable publication.

Daniel Rochatka is the inventor. No patent number has been assigned at this stage. A provisional application establishes a priority date while the full application is prepared.

The [Vocarum project page](/projects/vocarum/) and the published preprint ([Vocarum: A Truth Engine Built from Model Disagreement](/research/vocarum/)) describe the system in detail.
