This peer-reviewed study, published in *Biomedicines* (MDPI, September 2025), investigates how abnormal fetal and neonatal lung development presents as observable functional cardiac abnormalities during the third trimester. It forms part of a body of research on fetal echocardiography and systemic dysfunction from the same clinical research group whose placental thickness work (Journal of Clinical Medicine, 2025) acknowledged Daniel Rochatka's contribution.

No authorship or acknowledged contribution by Daniel Rochatka has been verified in the published record for this article. It is listed here as related biomedical research by the same group.

**Byline authors:** Julia Murlewska, Oskar Sylwestrzak, Maciej Słodki, Iwona Strzelecka, Łukasz Sokołowski, Monika Wójtowicz-Marzec, Iwona Maroszyńska, Ewa Cichos, Hanna Romanowicz, Maria Respondek-Liberska.
