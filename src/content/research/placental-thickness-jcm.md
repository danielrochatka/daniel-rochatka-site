This peer-reviewed study, published in the *Journal of Clinical Medicine* (MDPI, October 2025), investigates the relationship between placental thickness measured in the third trimester and a severity-weighted composite index of fetal cardiac and systemic dysfunction. The research addresses whether a sonographic marker as accessible as placental thickness can serve as a clinically meaningful indicator of the degree of fetal compromise.

Daniel Rochatka is acknowledged in the published article for assistance with calculations and development of data graphics. This is an acknowledged scientific contribution, not byline authorship.

**Byline authors:** Julia Murlewska, Oskar Sylwestrzak, Iwona Strzelecka, Łukasz Sokołowski, Paulina Kordjalik, Maciej Słodki, Maria Respondek-Liberska.
