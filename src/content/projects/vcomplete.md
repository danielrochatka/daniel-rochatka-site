Most important organizational information — policies, procedures, contracts, eligibility rules, structured data assets — changes constantly but has no reliable revision model. Files get overwritten, copies multiply with unclear authority, and the reasoning behind changes disappears.

VComplete applies the core discipline of version control — named revisions, comparison, review, restoration — to the documents and structured information that non-developers actually work with. The experience is designed so that users can create a controlled working copy, make and record changes, compare against prior versions, and return to a trusted state, without needing to understand branches, commits, or command-line tools.

The product direction includes both unstructured documents and structured business knowledge such as JSON records, decision tables, and machine-readable rules. The underlying version and provenance layer is designed to serve other applications through an API, not only a first-party editor.

The project is in incubation. Further detail is on the [Procyonsoft project page](https://procyonsoft.com/projects/vcomplete/).
