PersonalGuard addresses a real but underserved problem: personal digital communications remain fragmented, noisy, and poorly prioritized, yet the available tools require significant manual effort to manage or compromise user privacy to do so.

The product direction starts with intelligent email management — filtering by relevance and urgency, drafting context-aware replies, routing communication through controlled proxy identities — and extends to a broader personal intelligence layer that preserves continuity across tasks, reminders, and responsibilities without micromanagement.

Privacy is a design constraint, not an afterthought. The architecture is intended to favor user-controlled data, clear retention and deletion controls, and minimal exposure of private content to third-party training pipelines.

The project is in incubation. Further detail is on the [Procyonsoft project page](https://procyonsoft.com/projects/personalguard/).
